    Who We Are

    As part of the renowned Dutton Group, we have a well-established history that dates back to 1911. Dutton One is for the middle-tier consumer — we disrupt the market by making prestige cars accessible to more people. We have 17 thriving dealerships and counting. You’ll find Dutton One across Queensland, New South Wales, Victoria, South Australia and the Australian Capital Territory.

    Our Workshop

    Our Melbourne-based workshop offers hassle-free servicing, from general motor repairs to roadworthy certificates. When you choose Dutton One, it’s the only choice you’ll have to make on your luxury car journey.

    Our Locations

    You’ll find one of our stylish indoor showrooms near you, offering a wide range of pre-owned prestige cars to view, try and buy. Visit us today.
